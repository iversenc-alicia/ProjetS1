Know us before contacting us ?

I am a first year student in the MMI Department in Montbéliard. This site was created as part of the first semester project. For this project, the subject imposed was feminism. I have been interested in the subject of feminism for a long time before that, so I was interested in working on this in a teaching context. Having done a degree in Japanese before reorienting in this BUT MMI where I was lucky to be accepted in, I have decided to share my knowledges by talking about feminism and the situation of women in Japan. As French people, it is often difficult to imagine the context of a country on the other side of the world. This site has as goal to talk about the situation of women in Japan from different angles.
