Japan’s feminism

When we talk about feminism, everyone seems to know what it is. However, in a context as particular as Japan, feminism has to adapt.
Can we really affirm that we know the feminism of a country that is at the other side of the planet ? To begin to know Japan’s feminism, we must start by knowing the history of the women from this country.
This very particular story leads to a different feminism from the one that we know in Occident. If you’re looking to know more about feminism or Japan, this website will meet your expectations.
